About the Project

Empowering Learners with Free Technology Resources

Tech Lie BT is an open learning platform created with the vision to make quality technical education accessible to everyone. Our mission is to remove the barriers of cost, location, and complexity by providing completely free, beginner-friendly, and advanced-level courses in the fields of technology and digital skills.

Instead of charging for knowledge, we act as a “vendor” of skills — offering practical, well-structured learning materials for students, job seekers, and professionals who want to stay ahead in the fast-changing tech world.

Our Core Approach

  • Simple, intuitive, and responsive design for seamless learning on any device
  • Courses designed like a marketplace — easy to browse, filter, and access
  • Focus on real-world applications, not just theory
  • Completely free with no hidden charges or trial periods

Technologies & Tools

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Hosting: Netlify for fast, reliable global access
  • Design: Mobile-first layout with clean typography and color contrast

Why Tech Lie BT?

The platform was born from the realization that millions of learners still struggle to get quality education due to high fees and lack of reliable sources. With Tech Lie BT, you get a professional learning experience without the financial burden.

Our Vision

To become a global hub where anyone, from any background, can start learning technology for free and turn their skills into real opportunities.
