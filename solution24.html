About the Project

Solution 24 – Your Free IT Learning Hub

Solution 24 was created with the mission to break the barrier between learners and technology by offering completely free, high-quality IT courses. Instead of selling products like a traditional vendor site, Solution 24 “vendors” knowledge—acting as a marketplace for skills that can help individuals grow in their professional and personal lives.

The idea originated from observing how many aspiring learners in rural and urban areas face limitations due to high course fees or lack of quality resources. This inspired me to create a platform that delivers value without cost, but with the same level of professionalism and user experience as paid platforms.

Design & Development Process

1. Research & Planning

I studied popular e-learning and vendor-style sites to merge the concept of online education with the simplicity of a marketplace layout. My goal was to make navigation feel like shopping—only instead of products, you browse courses.

2. UI/UX Design

  • Created a clean and visually appealing interface using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript
  • Mobile-first design ensuring full responsiveness across devices
  • Bold headings, well-structured course cards, and vendor-style category sections for quick access

3. Development

  • HTML5 for semantic structure and SEO-friendly layouts
  • CSS3 for styling, grid/flexbox layouts, and hover animations to make browsing engaging
  • JavaScript for interactive elements, navigation effects, and dynamic loading
  • Fully hosted on Netlify for fast global accessibility and zero downtime

4. Optimization

  • Minimized CSS and JavaScript for faster load times
  • Tested on multiple browsers and devices to ensure compatibility

Key Features

  • Free courses covering MS Office, Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Data Analytics, Networking, Graphic Design, and more
  • Category-based navigation designed like a vendor shop for faster course discovery
  • Clean & responsive design for learning on desktops, tablets, and mobiles
  • Fast hosting & deployment powered by Netlify

Outcome & Impact

Solution 24 received positive feedback from peers and early users for its easy-to-navigate design, professional look, and meaningful purpose. It is not just a website—it’s a learning hub for students, job seekers, and working professionals who want to upskill without worrying about financial constraints.

This project showcases my skills as a full-cycle web developer—from ideation, design, and coding to deployment and user experience optimization.
